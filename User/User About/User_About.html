Introduction

EV Charging Station App will help the users to see the live Traffic Status.
i.e number of Vehicles waiting to charge their battery in a particular
EV Station.It will help the user to save the time and plan accordingly..

How it is going to work?

Every EV Charging Station which are intrested in displaying their Traffic Status
in my App will be provided a personal User Id and than after they will have
to Register through my Website.After completing the Registration process their
Traffic Status with address details will be(automatically) updated in my app.

Steps :

1]Download the E-V Charging Station App
2]Do one time Registration with a personal Password and submit.
3]Input the Starting point and Destination in Map section and then
you can see which E-V Charging Stations are in your route.
4]Then after observing the stations in route you have to move to the Data
section and you will get the Traffic Status of the particular E-V Station

Results :

All EV Charging Stations coming in the route will be displayed on the screen
with their live Traffic Status and Address details with a road map.

Future Updates that will be done in the App

1] Auto Vehicle Counting sensor will be installed in every EV Charging Stations
those who have registered for the App.
As of now it is a Demo Project,it has to be done manually.

2] I am planning to give a option of Pre-Booking the Time Slot
